RMSE
===

# Root Mean Square Error

$ Ground~Truth := [Y_{g, i}] \\ $
$ Evaluated~Value:= [Y_{e, i}] \\ $
\
$ RMSE~(unit) = \sqrt{\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^{N}(y_{e, i} - y_{g, i})^2} \\ $
$ RMSE~(\%) = \frac{\sqrt{\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^{N}(y_{e, i} - y_{g, i})^2}}{\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^{N} y_{g, i}}\times 100 \\ $
